Here we met for the second time.

A lot of time had passed. We had both become greatly different people, we just didn't know it yet.

It all seemed so effortless, like it clicked as easily and quickly as it had before.

Even though you thought I was an asshole (and rightly so) I'm so grateful every day that you have me another chance.

This place might not have much significance to you, to us, but I can't help but to feel grateful everytime I walk by here.
